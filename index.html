Tech Live

TechLive club’s motive is to exhilarate and excite young minds, to tingle their grey cells with a paragon of opportunity to showcase their talents and ideas which may lay the groundwork for the next cutting-edge technology.










TechLive club emphasizes on imagination and innovation to hone students’ skill by conducting various kinds of events. It is an area to device a devious play which pulls you form behind the scenes to the lead, where you can be dexterous, exhibitionist and good enough to outshine others.

TechLive has grown since the time of it’s inception and had included many fun-filled events with the aim of developing interest and encouraging innovation in technology among students.

We always try to come up with new ideas and with the theme of change, which helps the students to unveil their hidden talents. Events like code crackers, student of the year, flying finger, techno calypso, LAN Gaming etc. has made a great impact on the students and helps them to know their own hidden talents. And as the token of encouragement the students had been felicitated with the certificates as well as the cash prizes.

The role of the Tech Live club is to enhance the technical skills of students from various courses, including technical and non-technical branch. We organize various such activities so that students participate in a huge number.

TechLive is the technical club formed under the umbrella of Department of Computer Applications. The club organizes technical as well as fun events. It provides a platform for students to learn and enhance their technical skills.
